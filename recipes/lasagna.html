Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made of layered pasta sheets, rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. It's baked to perfection, offering a delicious combination of flavors and textures.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or sausage
  • Tomato sauce (or marinara)
  • Onion and garlic (chopped)
  • Mozzarella cheese (shredded)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Prepare the meat sauce:
    • Heat olive oil in a pan, sauté chopped onion and garlic.
    • Add ground beef or sausage and cook until browned.
    • Pour in tomato sauce, add Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  2. prepare the cheese mixture:
    • In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, an egg, and a portion of grated Parmesan cheese. Set aside.
  3. Cook the lasagna noodles
  4. Assemble the lasagna:
    • In a baking dish, spread a layer of meat sauce.
    • Add a layer of cooked noodles, followed by the ricotta mixture, and shredded mozzarella.
  5. Bake:
    • Cover with foil and bake in a preheated oven at 375°F (190°C) for 30 minutes.
