Banana pancakes

Description

Banana pancakes are a delicious and fluffy breakfast dish made by mixing mashed bananas into a pancake batter, adding natural sweetness and a soft texture. They are easy to make and can be enjoyed with toppings like honey, syrup, or fresh fruit.

Ingredients

  • Ripe bananas (mashed)
  • flour
  • Baking powder
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Sugar (optional)
  • Butter or oil (for cooking)

Steps

  1. Mash the bananas in a bowl.
    • Add eggs, milk, vanilla extract, and mix well.
  2. In another bowl:
    • combine flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  3. Mix the wet and dry ingredients to form a smooth batter.
  4. Heat a pan, grease with butter, and pour batter to form pancakes.
  5. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook until golden brown.
